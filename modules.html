Social Computing Learning Modules

We have a wide range of topics within social computing below. Please refer to each lesson's page for more information and resources!

Data Management

Students will understand their online presence and the impact of their digital footprint.

Online Behavior

Students will understand how their choices and interactions in online environments affect their lives.

HCI Careers

Students will understand the different careers in human-computer interaction and social computing.

Machine Learning & Bias

Students will understand what machine learning is, and the underlying biases that can affect them.

Encryption

Students will understand encryption and how it contributes to online security.

Misinformation

Students will understand how misinformation can be distributed and identified.
